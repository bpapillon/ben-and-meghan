The Weekend

The Floydfest site will be open from Friday, August 19 to Sunday, August 21 to guests who are camping, and we encourage our guests to bring a tent or RV and make a weekend of it. There will be clean bathrooms and (cold) showers on site, as well as water and power hookups for RVs.

Dinner will be served on Friday night for those staying on-site. You're on your own for late night snacks and any other meals; we recommend Dino's in Willis and Dogtown Roadhouse in Floyd.

Local Accomodations

Our recommended hotels in Floyd have reached capacity. For those not staying on site, we now recommend one of the many rental properties available on Airbnb.

There are also hotels available in Blacksburg and Roanoke, which are both about an hour's drive from the wedding site. We are currently researching options in these locations and will add recommendations here when we have them.
